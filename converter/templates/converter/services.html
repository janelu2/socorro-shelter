ABOUT ADOPTIONS

The city of socorro does NOT profit from animal adoptions. At the time of adoption, the city requires a deposit that goes towards the spay/neuter/vaccinations of your pet. The city does not make ANY money off of the animals. Once you provide proof the animals has been spayed or neutered, your deposit is refunded 100%.

Dogs: $90 neuter deposit (males) and $100 spay deposit (females)
Cats: $60 neuter deposit (males) and $80 spay deposit (females)
Sterilized Animals: Adoption fees will range from $20 to $100 depending on if it's a dog or a cat and if the animal was already sterilized when it enetered the shelter or if the animal was sterilized after entering the shelter.
