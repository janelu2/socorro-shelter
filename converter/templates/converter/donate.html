Donations

We appreciate donations any time of the year. Donated items can be brought to our shelter during regular business hours. No appointment is necessary.

We accept the following items:

  • Cat scratchers
  • Dog beds
  • Cat beds
  • Exercise pens
  • Newspapers
  • Canned tuna
  • Dish soap
  • Sponges
  • Kleenex
  • Collapsible cat tunnels


We CANNOT accept the following items:

  • Homemade dog treats
  • Old computer equipment
  • Medical supplies
  • Medication
  • Pesticide products
  • Used litter boxes
  • Old clothing
  • Sheets, pillows, pillow cases, comforters
  • Raw hides
  • Anything broken or missing pieces


How to volunteer:

We love our volunteers! Come down to our shelter to see what you can do to help us out. Some of the things you can expect to do when volunteering include:

  • Walking dogs
  • Washing dogs
  • Cleaning pens
  • Playing with cats
  • Cleaning litter boxes
